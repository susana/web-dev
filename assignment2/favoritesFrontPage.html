Good reads

"A good book should leave you... slightly exhausted at the end. You live several lives while reading it."

Books are doors to other lives, times and universes. Fantasy novels in particular take us to places that don't exist within or as part of our waking world. Some are skeptical of fantasy novels, citing unoriginality and predictability as the genre's greatest flaw. To this I say: "you haven't looked hard or long enough!" Thus it is my pleasure to share with you my top 5 favorite fantasy books in hopes that you may discover (and enjoy) exciting new adventures, endearing characters and complex plots.
